Purposes of My Personal Blog

    The purpose of my blog was opened to inform my users about current technology, mobile, entrepreneurship, digital, software and e-commerce issues. My blog contains last-minute articles on a variety of topics. My users have information about daily life thanks to current news and articles. I aim to become a popular blog page by increasing the number of readers and writers. In addition, there are about me and my projects content in my personal blog. Thanks to these contents, I opened my personal blog site in order to better introduce myself to users and convey my knowledge and experience.

Target Audience of My Personal Blog

    The target audience of my blog consists of communities or groups who want to have information about current technology, mobile, entrepreneurship, digital, software and e-commerce issues and are interested in these issues.

Reasons for My Personal Blog

    People who have knowledge about current technology, mobile, entrepreneurship, digital, software and e-commerce issues and are interested in these issues register on my blog site. According to the user role I give to registered users, they can spend time on my blog. Users in the standard member role cannot share their own blogs, but can read previously shared author blogs. Users in the role of authors can both share their own content and follow previously shared content. The most important reason for my blog is to enrich my blog site in terms of content thanks to the articles shared by my users, who are in the role of authors, by benefiting from their extensive knowledge and experience.

Users of My Personal Blog

    The users of my blog consist of people who are registered to my blog. There are 5 users on my blog. My users can comment on my blog as they wish. According to their user role, they can share their own blog posts on my blog.
